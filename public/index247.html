What To Do In An Emergency

Your actions right after an incident are critical. Follow these steps.

1
Stay Safe & Get Help

Ensure you are safe. If you are injured, call 911 immediately. Your health is the first priority.

2
Document Everything

Take photos of the scene, your injuries, and any documents. Get names and numbers of witnesses.

3
Call Us 24/7 (Do Not Talk)

Do NOT give a statement to police or insurance. Call us immediately. We are available 24/7 to protect your rights.
