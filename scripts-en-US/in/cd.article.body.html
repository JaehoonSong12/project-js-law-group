Your Rights Are Paramount

Your constitutional rights are always in effect, and this includes when you get arrested and are held for questioning. Understanding them is your first line of defense.

  • Fourth Amendment Protection Against Unreasonable Searches and Seizures
  • Fifth Amendment Right to Remain Silent
  • Sixth Amendment Right to Legal Counsel and to Be Informed of the Charges
