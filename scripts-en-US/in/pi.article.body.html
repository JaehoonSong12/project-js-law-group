What Constitutes a Claim?

If you need to seek compensation for emotional, physical, or psychological harm done by another party, then our team of attorneys is here for you. If the other party committed harm due to negligence, recklessness, or intentional acts, then you might have a claim. When it was their actions - or their lack of actions - that was the direct cause of your pain, then they are potentially liable for the damage that you took. If your situation fits these criteria, then schedule a free consultation with us now to see what we can do for you.
