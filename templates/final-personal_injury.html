Understanding Personal Injury Claims

Personal injury law (tort law) allows an injured plaintiff to get compensation when someone else's wrongful conduct (negligence or intentional act) causes harm. There are a variety of different situations that can give rise to a legitimate personal injury claim.

Common Types of Damages

  • Medical Expenses: Past, current, and future medical bills related to the injury.
  • Lost Wages: Income lost due to inability to work.
  • Pain and Suffering: Compensation for physical pain and emotional distress.
  • Property Damage: Cost to repair or replace damaged property (like a vehicle).
